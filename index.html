Project

Hotel Management System

The Hotel Management System Website is a web-based application designed to streamline and automate various processes within a hotel environment. The website is built using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure a responsive and visually appealing user interface. The system provides a range of features to enhance the efficiency of hotel operations, including:

Read more
  • User Authentication: Secure login and authentication mechanisms for staff members and administrators to access the system.
  • Room Booking and Reservation: A user-friendly interface for guests to browse available rooms, make reservations, and view booking details.
  • Admin Dashboard: An intuitive dashboard for hotel administrators to manage room inventory, check-in/check-out status, and monitor overall hotel performance.
  • Customer Management: A centralized database to store and manage customer information, including guest profiles and booking history.
  • Billing and Invoicing: Automated billing and invoicing system to generate and manage invoices for guests, including any additional services or charges.
  • Room Status Tracking: Real-time tracking of room availability, occupancy, and maintenance status to ensure efficient room turnover.
  • Feedback and Reviews: A feature for guests to provide feedback and reviews, helping the hotel to maintain and improve its services.
  • Responsive Design: Utilization of Bootstrap to create a responsive and mobile-friendly interface, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices.
  • Reports and Analytics: Generation of reports and analytics to provide insights into key performance indicators, helping hotel management make informed decisions.
